About us

Westhorpe Bespoke is a Suffolk based company, who specialize in bespoke kitchen installations. The whole process is made easy, with everything from removal of old to fitting of new made quick and easy. Prided on their quality of work and absolute reliability; their customers can be assured that they are getting an excellent finish from a professional company.
